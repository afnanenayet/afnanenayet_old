Minigma

Minigma is an app with an in-house encryption engine. A seed is generated based on a user input which creates a cypher which can be used to encode/decode messages, salting is used as well, to obfuscute the cipher. All encryption is local, requiring no internet access, so that communications can't be intercepted in the cloud.

The program generates a seed based on a user input (the password). This password is converted to an integer which is used as the seed for the pseudo-random generator which manipulates an array to create a cipher. A "standard" cipher is used to convert the characters entered by the user into the corresponding characters in the generated cipher. This places the entire burden of security on the password rather than the system.

Battery Informatics

Battery Informatics is a utility that provides information about the battery of an Android phone. It informs a user about the state of their battery, the amount of current flowing in (or out), temperature, remaining capacity, and overall battery health.

This app utilizes proper Material Design guidelines, and is compatible with any Android device running Android 5.0 (API level 22) or above.

Loopr

Why pay for a pedal?

Loopr turns your phone into a free looper pedal. Place your phone by your amp, or if you have an amp that's compatible with USB (like the Fender Mustang), plug it into your phone directly. If you don't have a USB cable, just use your microphone. Record a few chords, and play over them. Don't limit yourself to a guitar - use this for beatboxing, or any sounds you want to play on repeat.
